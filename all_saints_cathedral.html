all saints cathedral

The All Saints Cathedral in Shillong, Meghalaya, stands as an architectural and spiritual marvel. Constructed during the British Raj in the early 20th century, this Anglican cathedral showcases a blend of colonial and indigenous architectural styles.

Nestled amidst the lush landscapes of Shillong, the cathedral's distinctive spire and stained glass windows create a captivating silhouette. Serving as the seat of the Bishop of Shillong, it holds significant religious importance. The serene ambiance within its walls, adorned with intricate woodwork and religious symbols, makes All Saints Cathedral a peaceful sanctuary for worshipers and a notable landmark in the charming hill station of Shillong.

Location: Sarojini Naidu Marg, Civil Lines, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211001

Timings: Open all year from 8 AM to 6 PM

How to Reach

By Flight

The nearest airport is Prayagraj Airport, approximately 12 km from All Saints Cathedral. Prayagraj Airport is well connected by railway networks with most of the cities of India with daily flights. From the airport, travelers can hire a taxi or use app-based cab services to reach the site.

By Train

Allahabad Railway Junction is about 2 km from All Saints Cathedral. It is well connected by railway networks with most of the cities of India. Trains to and from Allahabad Junction Railway Station are frequent. Upon arrival, visitors can hire a taxi or use app-based cab services to reach the site.

By Bus

The Civil Lines Bus Stand is around 3 km from All Saints Cathedral. State-run buses and private operators connect Prayagraj to various cities. From the bus stand, local transport options like auto-rickshaws or app-based cab services are available to reach the destination.
