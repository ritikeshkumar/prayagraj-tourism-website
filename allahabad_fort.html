allahabad fort

Built-in 1583, this stunning piece of architecture stands at the Triveni Sangam in Allahabad to remind tourists of the city’s rich history. Akbar, a famous Mughal Emperor, is said to have been much impressed by the aura of Allahabad. As a result, he decided to construct a grand fort in the region. This fort was erected to envelop the Akshayavat, a holy tree for the Hindus of the area.

The Archaeological Survey of India has classified the Allahabad fort as a monument of National Importance and maintains its interiors for tourists. The exteriors reflect the brilliance of Mughal architecture with significant motifs and inscriptions in some parts of the fort. As a tourist, do not forget to enjoy the grandeur of the fort while in Allahabad, since it is one of the best places to visit in prayagraj.

Location: Allahabad fort, Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh 211005

Timings: 9:44 AM to 6 PM

How to Reach

By Flight

The airport nearest to allahabad fort is Prayagraj Airport, approximately 15 km from Allahabad Fort. Prayagraj Airport is well connected by railway networks with most of the cities of India with daily flights. From the airport, travelers can hire a taxi or use app-based cab services to reach the site.

By Train

Allahabad Railway Junction is about 8 km from Allahabad Fort. It is well connected by railway networks with most of the cities of India. Trains to and from Allahabad Junction Railway Station are frequent. Upon arrival, visitors can hire a taxi or use app-based cab services to reach the site.

By Bus

The Civil Lines Bus Stand is approximately 10 km from Allahabad Fort. State-run buses and private operators connect Prayagraj to various cities. From the bus stand, local transport options like auto-rickshaws or app-based cab services are available to reach the destination.
